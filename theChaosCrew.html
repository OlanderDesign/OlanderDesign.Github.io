the Creed

the Purpose

The Chaos Crew exists to solve problems and create value in a rapidly changing and unpredictable world. We thrive on chaos and uncertainty, and approach every challenge with agility, creativity, and resilience.

the 5 Values

  1. Agility: We are quick to adapt to changing circumstances and approach problems with a flexible, agile mindset. We value the ability to pivot and adjust our approach as needed to achieve our goals.
  2. Creativity: We approach problems with an open mind and a willingness to think outside the box. We value creativity and innovation, and are constantly seeking new and better ways to solve problems.
  3. Resilience: We don't give up in the face of adversity. We value the ability to bounce back from setbacks, learn from our failures, and keep moving forward.
  4. Collaboration: We believe that working together is key to achieving success. We value collaboration and teamwork, and strive to create an environment where everyone's contributions are respected and valued.
  5. Optimism: We maintain a positive attitude, even in the face of uncertainty and chaos. We value the ability to stay optimistic and focused on finding solutions, and believe that a positive mindset can make all the difference in achieving our goals.

the 10 Rules
