Idea Canvas

Welcome to your new favorite tool for sparking creativity and structuring your ideas! Inspired by the best in innovation thinking like Innovator's DNA, INSEAD's Age of Disruption, Strategyzer, and Lean Canvas the Idea Canvas is here to help you dream big and think smart.

What's Inside

Inspiration: Look beyond your bubble. Gather fresh insights and trends from the world around you.
Insights: Dive deep into your own experiences and knowledge. Your best ideas often come from within.
Change: Spot the challenges ahead. Knowing your obstacles helps you plan your path.
HMW...?: Frame your challenges with powerful questions. They guide your thinking and open up new possibilities.
Options: Explore different routes. Innovation is all about finding the best way forward.


How to Use It

Focus on One Idea: Hone in on a specific idea and develop it thoroughly.
Brainstorm Freely: Use the canvas to explore different areas and find patterns.
Build a Database: Organize your findings to make new connections and insights.


Mindset

Remember, the goal isn't to fill out every section. It's about staying aware of potential blind spots and making cross-connections. The Idea Canvas is designed to be used before other tools to refine a specific idea and long after, as a data collection tool for discovering new combinations. The canvas is available as an SVG file, perfect for MIRO, printing on posters, or adding to PowerPoint presentations.
